Privacy Policy for Tennis Score Meter

Last Updated: May 1, 2026

programazer.com operates the Tennis Score Meter mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent experience for all users.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our App is designed to function entirely offline. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information from your device on external servers.

  • Permissions: The App requests zero system permissions (such as Location, Camera, or Contacts).
  • User-Provided Data: Names for players or teams are stored locally on your device's internal storage. This data is used exclusively to display scores and generate match reports within the App.

2. Third-Party Service Providers

The App is built using standard Android Jetpack libraries and Material 3 components. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs (such as AdMob), or cloud-based backend services that track user behavior or collect data.

3. PDF Reports and Scoped Storage

The App includes a "Report Match" feature that generates PDF documents.

  • Local Generation: Reports are created locally using Android's standard file-saving APIs (Scoped Storage).
  • Storage Path: Reports are typically saved to your device's Downloads/TennisScoreMeter folder.
  • Data Control: We have no access to these files. You have full control to view, share, or delete them via your device's File Manager.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If we discover that a child under 18 has provided us with personal data, we immediately delete this from our local internal storage. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

5. Data Deletion

Because no data is stored on our servers, you can delete all local App data by:

  • Clearing the App’s cache/data in your Android device settings.
  • Uninstalling the App.
  • Manually deleting generated PDF reports using a File Manager.

6. Security

We value your trust. Since the App does not transmit data over the internet, your information is protected from remote unauthorized access by the built-in security features of the Android operating system.
